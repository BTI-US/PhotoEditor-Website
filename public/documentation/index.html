Features

PhotoEditor is a powerful Android application that allows you to edit and enhance your photos with ease. It offers a wide range of features, including filters, effects, cropping, resizing, and more.

To get started, download and install the PhotoEditor app from the Google Play Store. Once installed, open the app and select a photo from your gallery or take a new one. You can then use the various editing tools to adjust your photo to your liking. When you're done, save your edited photo to your device or share it directly from the app.

Activation Code Acquisition

To unlock the advanced features of our app, you need to acquire an activation code. Follow the instructions below:

  1. Obtain activation code -
    1. Buy our token from the designated platform.
    2. Receive a hash code as the activation code.
    3. Store it securely somewhere.


  1. Activation code verification -
    1. Open the APP and click the `activation` button.
    2. Enter this hash code into the activation field in our app.
    3. Click 'verify' to verify the activation code
    4. Once the code is verified success fully, the advance feature of editing will be unlocked.
